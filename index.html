MODERN DIGGER

Dig through the terrain, collect treasures, and defeat enemies in this modern twist on the classic digger game!

How to Play:

  • Arrow Keys or Touch Controls to move your character
  • Spacebar or Fire Button to shoot
  • Collect Emeralds and Gold Bags for points
  • Use Power-ups to gain special abilities
  • Avoid enemies or destroy them with your bullets
  • Complete each level by collecting all emeralds
