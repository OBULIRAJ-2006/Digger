CLASSIC DIGGER GAME

DIG FOR TREASURE!

Collect gold and emeralds, avoid monsters!

HOW TO PLAY

  • Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move
  • Dig through dirt to collect gold and emeralds
  • Shoot enemies with the FIRE button
  • Avoid enemies or you'll lose a life
  • Push gold bags into enemies to crush them
  • Collect all emeralds to complete levels
  • Complete all 3 levels to win!
